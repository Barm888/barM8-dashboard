Welcome to Bar M8

We have done the hard work and listed many venues on the App.
To find out if your venue is listed, Enter the landline number of your
Venue including area code e.g. 0286287500 without spaces or brackets.
If your Venue is listed, you will be directed to the Profile Page.
Complete the Account Details section to Activate the account.
If Venue is NOT listed, you will be directed to Registration Page.
